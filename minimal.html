What is minimal fitness?

Minimal fitness is not a new fad, crazy approach to training or some absured and complicated trend. Minimal fitness in short is a simple approach to fitness. You take away the clutter of highly saturated fitness industry and strip all the info down to the essentials, the key things you need to truly see results. "The Holy Trinity" of fitness so to speak that have been lost in translation. These mainly being Diet, Training and Rest. These 3 vital key elements can make or break your results in and outside of the gym. My aim is too help clarify and educate you so that you can either begin your fitness journey, or if you already are get you back on track! This is minimal fitness!
