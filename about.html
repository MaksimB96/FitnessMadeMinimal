What is minimal fitness?

Minimal fitness is not a fad of some sorts. It's not an unrealistic training routine. It's not a over-complicated fitness concept. Minimal fitness is what you need when you need it. It strips away all the complication from todays over-saturated fitness industry, and provides you with the percise knowledge that you need in order to excel in your fitness journey. Whether you're a complete beginner, or you have already taken your first steps on your fitness journey, these bits of information will propel you forwards. There are 3 main elements to the minimal approach. Diet, Training and Rest. That's it! That's all it takes in order to see good results.

However, as mentioned before, the fitness industry is over saturated with informtion. Therefore I'm going to break these core elements down into simple, easy to follow chunks of information that will start you off on the right path. Fitness shouldn't be complicated. As long as you put in the work and stay consistant and approach fitness minimally you will get maximum results.
